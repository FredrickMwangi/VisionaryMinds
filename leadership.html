Executive Team

Meet the Team

Passionate, accountable, and community-focused — our leaders set the tone for everything Visionary Minds stands for.

Jane Wanjiku Kamau

Chairlady

Jane is a passionate advocate for women's empowerment and community development with over 8 years of grassroots leadership experience. She co-founded Visionary Minds in 2019 with a clear vision: to create opportunities for every young person and woman in her community to thrive.

Under her leadership, the organisation has grown from a small savings group to a multi-program CBO serving hundreds of community members. She holds a Diploma in Community Development and is currently pursuing a Degree in Social Work.

Nairobi, Kenya
+254 700 000 001

Mary Achieng Otieno

Secretary

Mary brings exceptional organisational skills and a meticulous approach to records management and communication. She has been with Visionary Minds since its founding, ensuring all governance processes, member records, and correspondence are handled with the highest level of professionalism.

She holds a Certificate in Business Administration and has completed training in non-profit governance and project management. Mary is also an active facilitator of the Digital Skills program.

Nairobi, Kenya
secretary@visionaryminds.or.ke

Ruth Njeri Kamau

Treasurer

Ruth is a certified accountant committed to the financial transparency and accountability of Visionary Minds. She manages all finances, donor reporting, and budget planning with precision and integrity, ensuring every shilling goes toward maximum community impact.

She holds a CPA (K) qualification and has experience in NGO financial management. Ruth is also the lead facilitator of the Financial Literacy program, mentoring members on personal and group financial management.

Nairobi, Kenya
