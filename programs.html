Our Programs

Program 01

Financial Literacy & Savings Groups

Our Financial Literacy program equips community members — particularly women and youth — with the knowledge and skills needed to make informed financial decisions. Through table banking, savings circles, and structured workshops, participants gain practical experience managing money, accessing credit, and building financial resilience.

  • Weekly savings group formation and facilitation
  • Budget creation and money management workshops
  • Table banking and internal micro-lending schemes
  • Investment basics and financial goal-setting sessions
  • Credit score awareness and formal banking orientation

Program 02

Youth Entrepreneurship Bootcamp

The Entrepreneurship Bootcamp is a 12-week intensive program designed to transform ideas into viable businesses. Participants receive mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs, develop bankable business plans, and are linked to seed-funding opportunities and market networks.

  • Business ideation, validation, and planning workshops
  • Pitch training and investor readiness sessions
  • Access to micro-finance and start-up seed grants
  • Market linkage and customer acquisition strategies
  • Post-incubation support and alumni network access

Program 03

Leadership Development Program

The Leadership Development Program cultivates the next generation of community leaders through structured training, peer mentoring, and civic engagement activities. Participants develop communication, problem-solving, and governance skills that enable them to lead change in their communities.

  • Leadership styles and governance principles workshops
  • Public speaking and communication skills training
  • Community project design and management
  • Civic education and rights awareness sessions
  • Youth leadership forums and networking events

Program 04

Digital Skills for the Future

In an increasingly digital world, we ensure our community members are not left behind. Our Digital Skills program provides foundational computer literacy up to advanced training in digital marketing, freelancing, and online entrepreneurship, opening new economic pathways for youth.

  • Computer basics and internet literacy for beginners
  • Microsoft Office Suite and productivity tools training
  • Digital marketing — SEO, social media, content creation
  • Online freelancing platforms (Upwork, Fiverr, remote work)
  • E-commerce setup and mobile money integration

Program 05

Community Development Initiatives

Community development is at the heart of everything we do. Through participatory planning and collective action, we partner with community members to identify local needs and co-create solutions — from environmental clean-ups to infrastructure advocacy and social cohesion activities.

  • Environmental clean-up and tree-planting drives
  • Community needs assessment and participatory planning
  • Infrastructure advocacy with county government
  • Neighbourhood watch and safety initiatives
  • Health awareness campaigns and social events

Program 06

Mentorship & Career Guidance

Our Mentorship program pairs young community members with experienced professionals for one-on-one guidance, career mapping, and personal development. Through structured mentoring cycles, we accelerate individual growth and help participants make informed decisions about their futures.

  • One-on-one professional mentorship matching
  • Career exploration and guidance sessions
  • CV writing, interview preparation, and job readiness
  • Life skills training — goal setting, time management
  • Alumni network events and peer accountability groups
