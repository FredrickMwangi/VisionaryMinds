FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member of Visionary Minds?

You can join Visionary Minds by calling or emailing us directly using the contact details above. Membership is open to youth (18–35) and women in Nairobi. There is a nominal registration fee which goes toward running our programs.

Are the training programs free for members?

Most of our programs are heavily subsidised or free for registered members, made possible through donor support. Some specialist workshops may have a small participation fee. Contact us for the current fee schedule.

Can organisations partner with Visionary Minds?

Yes! We actively seek partnerships with NGOs, corporations, government agencies, and faith-based organisations. Reach out by email or phone and tell us about your organisation and intended collaboration.

How can I donate to Visionary Minds?

We accept donations via M-PESA, bank transfer, and in-kind contributions. Contact our treasurer directly for donation details. All funds are used transparently and donors receive annual impact reports.

Where are your meetings held?

Our weekly community meetings and training sessions are held at various community centres in Nairobi. The specific venue is shared with members via our WhatsApp group. Contact us to be added to the group.
