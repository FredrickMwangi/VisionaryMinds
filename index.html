Who We Are

A Community of
Change Makers

Visionary Minds Community Based Organisation (CBO) is a registered non-profit dedicated to the holistic development of marginalised youth and women in Kenya.

We work at the grassroots level, partnering with local leaders, government agencies, and international organisations to implement programs that create lasting, measurable change in the communities we serve.
